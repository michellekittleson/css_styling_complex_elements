About Me

My interest in decorating cakes began in 2001, when I started the tradition of a Super Bowl cake. This was my only real cake endeavor until summer 2019, when I made a rainbow explosion cake to bring to a Pride celebration. Since then, I've enjoyed making and decorating cakes to celebrate the special occasions of friends and family.
